Republic of Congo

The Republic of the Congo is located in Central Africa. It is a very beautiful country where life is pleasant, and the population is kind and hospitable. It is also a country known for its good cuisine, love of art, and fashion. One of the greatest peculiarities of the Congo is the emphasis on environmental protection; despite their urbanization, the cities maintain trees in every corner, making the air in city centers as breathable as it is in the countryside. The Congo also has many sites that attract a good number of tourists, including its cultural places—yes! The Congo and its people particularly cherish their cultures and roots. There is also its magnificent river, located in its capital: Brazzaville, and its port located in its economic capital: Pointe-Noire.
